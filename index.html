WOMEN'S LIFESTYLE

Fashion Trends

Pack away the leggings: the social and sartorial hiatus is officially over. Yes, dressing up is the new dressing down, minimalism is dead, skirts are teeny-weeny and heels are huge. Example A: Dua Lipa owning the Versace catwalk to the sound of her own music. Sure, the outfit is good but the attitude is even better. Meanwhile at Chanel models not only smiled for the cameras, they twirled too, sashaying their way through the show in a manner last seen in the 1990s. In short, sass is in — say hello to fashion’s cheekiest season yet.
